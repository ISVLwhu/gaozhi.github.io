Zhi Gao Ph.D

Professor Zhi Gao is a professor at the School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering, Wuhan University. His main research interests include artificial intelligence, computer vision, and remote sensing for intelligent unmanned reconnaissance systems. Previously, he was a research scientist at Temasek Laboratories, National University of Singapore. He received his Ph.D. from Wuhan University.
